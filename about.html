Why I Write

The Weight of Knowing began as a place to document lessons that changed me. Not because I have all the answers, but because writing helps me understand the questions.

This journal explores reflection, accountability, relationships, resilience, growth, ambition, and the quiet moments that shape who we become.

If even one essay helps someone pause, think differently, or feel less alone, then this project has served its purpose.
